Lasagna

Description

In this recipe we're going to be making a Lasagna

Making Lasagna can be very time consuming but it is worth it trust me. If you like cheese it's going to be right for you

Ingredients
  • Meat:Sweet Italian sausage and ground beef
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomato products:A can of crushed tomatoes, two cans of tomato sauce and two cans of tomato paste
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seosonings:Fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds and black pepper
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses:Parmesan, mozzarella and ricotta cheese
  • Egg
